# Test Cases — E-commerce

## TC-01 Add Product to Cart
**Steps:**
1. Open the product listing page
2. Select a product
3. Click on "Add to Cart"

**Expected Result:**
The selected product is added to the cart successfully.

---

## TC-02 Remove Product from Cart
**Steps:**
1. Add a product to the cart
2. Open the cart
3. Click on "Remove"

**Expected Result:**
The product is removed from the cart.

---

## TC-03 Update Product Quantity
**Steps:**
1. Add a product to the cart
2. Open the cart
3. Increase the quantity of the product

**Expected Result:**
The cart updates the quantity and total price correctly.

---

## TC-04 Checkout with Valid Data
**Steps:**
1. Add a product to the cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Fill in valid shipping and payment information
4. Confirm the purchase

**Expected Result:**
The order is placed successfully.

---

## TC-05 Checkout with Empty Required Fields
**Steps:**
1. Add a product to the cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Leave required fields empty
4. Try to confirm the purchase

**Expected Result:**
The system displays validation messages and prevents checkout.
